FAQ

Can I cancel my order?

Yes, you can cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it. After that, it may not be possible to cancel.

I put in the wrong address for my order, what should i do?

You can update your order details within 24 hours by contacting customer service. Changes may not be possible after processing begins.

I have not recieved my order comfirmation what should i do?

If you haven’t received your order confirmation, please check your spam or junk mail folder. If it’s not there, contact our customer support team for assistance.

How can i check the status of my order?

You can check the status of your order by logging into your account on our website or by using the tracking information provided in your confirmation email.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer refunds under certain conditions. Please review our refund policy for detailed information, or contact customer support for assistance.

Can I update my order after placing it?

You can update your order details within 24 hours by contacting customer service. Changes may not be possible after processing begins.

How can I track my parcel?

You will receive a tracking number via email once your order has shipped. Use the tracking number on the carrier’s website to check the status of your parcel.

What happens if my order is delayed?

If your order is delayed, please contact our support team for further assistance. We’ll help resolve the issue promptly.

My items have arrived damaged what should i do?

If your items arrived damaged, please take photos of the damage and contact our customer support team immediately. We’ll assist you with a replacement or refund.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, PayPal, and other online payment options.

How can I request a refund?

To request a refund, contact customer service with your order number. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

Is my payment secure?

Yes, all payments are processed through encrypted channels for maximum security.

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and fill in your details to create an account.

What should I do if I forget my password?

Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset your password via email.

How can I update my account details?

Log in to your account, navigate to the settings page, and update your personal information as needed.

Are your products eco-friendly?

Yes, we ensure that most of our products are made using sustainable and eco-friendly materials.

Yes, all products come with a 1-year warranty. Please check individual product pages for more details.

Delivery times are estimated at checkout based on your location and shipping option selected.
